Rules

    Note: Use common sense and don't be a douche. There are more to "rules" than those written here. This is not meant as an exhaustive list. Listen to the staff

  • 1) Social
    • 1.1 Follow the Discord ToS
    • 1.2 No hate speech or harassment
    • 1.3 Keep the server PG-13. Keep in mind some of our members might be younger than you. Set an example
    • 1.4 Unwanted PvP (consentual is allowed), stealing and griefing are severly punished
    • 1.5 Don't spam in the Discord or on the server
    • 1.6 Respect other player's claims and boundaries. Do not use farms without explicit permission. It is generally accepted to harvest crops for food as long as you replant unless the owner decides otherwise. If a sign says "keep out", keep out
  • 2) Technical
    • 2.1 Any unofficial applications/mods/client giving you an advantage over other players are considered cheating and are strictly prohibited. In case of doubt, contact the staff
  • 3) Building
    • 3.1 Ask for permission from the players you want to settle nearby
    • 3.2 No building within 500 blocks from spawn. The area around spawn is dedicated to community builds such as the Shopping District and the Minigames District. Anything built prior to this rule is safe to stay
    • 3.3 See the Survival-Adverts channel for the Shopping District rules
    • 3.4 Mega-projects are to be validated by staff; if you plan a build spanning >= 50 chunks please contact staff for approval prior to building
    • 3.5 Spawnproof all builds on the nether roof
    • 3.6 Consider lag and server health when building your farms. For example:
      • 3.6.1 Put your villagers in minecarts or on double carpets to disable their pathfinding AI
      • 3.6.2 Put composters on your unlocked hoppers to disable their vacuum sucking action and reduce their impact on the server
